# CarbonLens Pitch Notes

## Problem

Companies are being pushed to track ESG performance and greenhouse gas emissions, but most still rely on fragmented spreadsheets, email follow-ups, and manual reconciliation across internal teams and suppliers. This leads to poor data quality, weak reporting confidence, and very limited climate accountability.

## Solution

CarbonLens is a smart ESG control tower for manufacturing SMEs. It centralizes emissions-related data, calculates Scope 1 and Scope 2 footprints, supports limited Scope 3 supplier input, flags data quality issues, and generates trusted sustainability summaries.

## Why It Matters

- ESG reporting is becoming commercially important
- Manufacturing companies face operational and supplier data complexity
- SMEs need practical tools, not heavyweight enterprise platforms
- Trustworthy carbon data is necessary for both compliance and planning

## What Makes CarbonLens Different

- It is not just a reporting dashboard
- It adds governance and ownership to missing or suspicious data
- It treats supplier Scope 3 participation as part of the workflow
- It keeps calculations transparent and explainable
- It is designed to be realistically adopted by SMEs
- It adds an AI-smart layer for insights without making AI the source of truth

## Hackathon Strength

- Strong end-to-end story
- Clear business value
- Practical scope
- Good visual demo potential
- Easy to explain in a short pitch
- Balances futuristic product feel with realistic execution

## Feasibility

- Focused MVP
- Seeded data support
- Low-infra stack
- Deterministic calculations
- AI only as an optional enhancement

## Closing Line

CarbonLens helps organizations move from scattered ESG reporting to reliable climate operations and decision-making.
