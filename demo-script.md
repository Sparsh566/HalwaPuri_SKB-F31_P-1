# CarbonLens Demo Script

## Goal

Deliver a strong 3 to 5 minute demo that shows the problem, the product flow, and the business impact without getting lost in technical detail.

## Opening

“Manufacturing companies today still manage ESG and GHG data across spreadsheets, emails, and supplier follow-ups. That makes reporting slow, unreliable, and hard to audit. CarbonLens solves this by becoming a single source of truth for emissions data, supplier engagement, and climate accountability.”

## Demo Flow

## Step 1: Show The Dashboard

Highlight:
- Total emissions
- Scope 1 and Scope 2 split
- Scope 3 coverage indicator
- Data quality score
- Open governance issues

Talk track:
“Right away, leadership gets a clear snapshot of the company’s carbon footprint and where data quality or accountability gaps still exist.”

## Step 2: Upload Activity Data

Highlight:
- Upload CSV for electricity or fuel records
- Validation preview
- Updated totals after import

Talk track:
“Instead of manually merging spreadsheets, teams can upload operational data directly and immediately see validated results.”

## Step 3: Supplier Scope 3 Submission

Highlight:
- Simple supplier form
- Submission completeness check
- Updated supplier contribution view

Talk track:
“Scope 3 is often the hardest part of ESG reporting. CarbonLens makes supplier participation simple and trackable.”

## Step 4: Governance And Issues

Highlight:
- Automatically flagged issue
- Owner assignment
- Status or escalation state

Talk track:
“This is where CarbonLens goes beyond dashboards. It adds accountability, so missing or suspicious data becomes a trackable business action.”

## Step 5: Generate Report

Highlight:
- Summary report page
- Executive insights
- Audit-ready narrative

Talk track:
“The result is a report-ready summary leadership can use for review, planning, and external reporting preparation.”

## Close

“CarbonLens turns scattered ESG and GHG inputs into a trusted, actionable system. It helps manufacturing SMEs move from manual reporting to real sustainability decision support.”

## Final Impact Line

“We’re not just measuring carbon. We’re making climate accountability operational.”
